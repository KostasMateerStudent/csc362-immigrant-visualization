Around the World

Visualization 1

A line chart of the number of nonimmigrant admissions into the US by continent between the years 2012-2021. The chart displays the trends of the number of visits by these workers to the US over time and the impact of COVID on it as well.

Visualization 2

A linear and logarithmic bar chart of the number of nonimmigrant workers who came into the US in 2021 by the continent that they came in from. This highlights how the majority of these workers come from North America while much fewer come from the rest of the world.

Visualization 3

A choropleth of the number of nonimmigrant workers who came into the US in 2021 by the state that they entered in. This highlights how the majority of these workers enter through Arizona, Michigan, California, Texas, Florida, and New York.
